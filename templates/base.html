Fine Print

I made this website for personal use, I modestly hope other people may find it useful. The information provided on this website is basically a summary of parts of the XML Path Language spec published by the W3C, available at the link below. I cannot guarantee that the information here is entirely accurate and it's definitely not an inclusive guide to every aspect of XPath. I've attempted to do a good job, but this is still very much a work in progress. There are too many intentional omissions and simplifications to mention.
